Welcome to Acorn

Acorn Industries is a business that focuses on welding. Owned and operated by Andrew Lange, Acorn is based in Canning Vale, Western Australia. Acorn services the Oil & Gas, Construction and Maintenance Industries. Acorn can offer welding services using a full range of welding processes across the three primary metal groups, Carbon steel, Stainless Steel & Aluminium. Acorn can assess the project our clients have at hand, propose a tailored solution to suit our clients satisfaction, then execute it, through understanding, knowledge & quality systems.
