Welcome to Acorn

Acorn Welding is owned and operated by Andrew Lange, we are based in Canning Vale, Western Australia. We service the Oil & Gas, Construction and Maintenance Industries. We can offer welding services using a full range of welding processes, across the three primary metal groups, Carbon steel, Stainless Steel & Aluminium. We can assess the project our clients have at hand, propose a tailored solution to suit our clients satisfaction, then execute it, through understanding, knowledge & quality systems.
